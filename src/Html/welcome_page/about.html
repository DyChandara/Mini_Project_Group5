About Nham To

Where fast meets fresh! Serving up Cambodia's most craveable fast food since 2019. From our kitchen to your table - every bite tells a story of quality, speed, and pure deliciousness.

The Fast Food Revolution Started Here

Back in 2019, we had a crazy idea - what if fast food could actually taste amazing? What if we used real ingredients, cooked everything fresh, and still got your order out lightning-fast?

That's how Nham To was born. We're not just another burger joint - we're the place where busy Cambodians come when they want something quick, delicious, and satisfying. Whether you're grabbing lunch between meetings or treating the family to dinner, we've got you covered.
